Inspire Try Outs

Inspire is our rocking cheer team for athletes with special needs. 2018-19 will mark our 8th season of Inspire, and we couldn’t be happier to have the opportunity to train and work with incredible athletes who truly are inspiring.

Being a part of Inspire lets your athlete train alongside other cheer kids, go to 3 local competitions, and perform at our Excite! Hometown Pep Rally in March!

Interested in becoming part of Inspire? Come by for try outs on Sunday, September 30th from 1:00 to 1:45 pm here at Excite!

We’ll have a parent meeting for the first 30 minutes.
